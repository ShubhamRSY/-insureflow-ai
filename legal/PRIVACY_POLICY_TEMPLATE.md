## What we collect

Account credentials, underwriting submission documents Customer uploads, job metadata, audit trails, and diagnostic telemetry needed to operate the service.

## How we use data

To provide underwriting pipelines, generate memos/quotes, maintain examiner audit packages, improve reliability, and meet legal obligations.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share with subprocessors (cloud hosting, optional LLM APIs) under contracts. Bank customers may require Customer-controlled AWS accounts.

## Security

TLS in transit; encryption at rest in bank mode; role-based access; optional SSO; CloudTrail/CloudWatch in AWS deployments.

## Retention

Operational logs per Customer policy; sealed WORM audits default ~7 years unless Customer instructs otherwise.
