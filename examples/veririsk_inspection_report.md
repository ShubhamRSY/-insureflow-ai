### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

PropGuard inspected both the Pearl District retail bakery listed on the broker application and a **second production facility** at 4820 SE 26th Ave operated by the same insured. Management confirmed the commissary produces 70% of wholesale volume and employs 18 of 28 total staff.

**Critical findings:**

1. **Undisclosed location** — The commissary at 4820 SE 26th Ave is not listed on the broker ACORD. This facility houses $680,000 in production equipment and represents the majority of TIV exposure.

2. **Sprinkler deficiency** — The commissary kitchen has Type I hood suppression over cooking line only. **No building sprinkler system.** A 2024 grease fire (Claim HART-2024-001903) occurred at this location. Application represents insured as fully sprinklered.

3. **Protection class mismatch** — Commissary is ISO Class 4, not Class 2 as implied by single-location application.

4. **Construction / occupancy** — Commissary is light steel frame with CMU block, built 2011. High-heat commercial ovens (4 deck ovens, 2 rack ovens) create elevated fire load.

### BUILDING CONSTRUCTION — PEARL DISTRICT

Two-story joisted masonry building in the Pearl District. Ground floor: retail cafe (1,200 sq ft) and display case. Second floor and basement: production kitchen (3,000 sq ft combined). Built 1924, renovated 2018. Roof: modified bitumen, replaced 2018, good condition.

Fire protection: Wet pipe sprinkler throughout. Central station alarm. Class K extinguisher at cooking line. Hood suppression over range.

### BUILDING CONSTRUCTION — COMMISSARY (4820 SE 26th Ave)

Single-story light industrial building, 7,600 sq ft clear span. Steel frame, CMU walls. Built 2011. Occupancy: commercial bakery production — wholesale bread, pastry, and catering prep.

**Fire protection:** Type I hood suppression over cooking line and ovens. **No sprinkler system.** Portable extinguishers only. Local smoke detection — not central station. Last NFPA 96 hood cleaning documented 11 months prior to survey (recommend quarterly).

Electrical: 400A service, panel in good condition. Production equipment on dedicated circuits. Extension cord observed at mixer station — corrected during survey.

### PRIOR LOSSES (field verification)

Surveyor cross-referenced maintenance records with VeriRisk loss feed:

### RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Require full sprinkler retrofit at commissary** or exclude location from property coverage until compliant
2. Add commissary to policy schedule and increase TIV accordingly
3. Obtain updated NFPA 96 cleaning contract (quarterly minimum)
4. Reconcile employee count and revenue with D&B vendor feed before binding
