## CLAIM DETAIL

### Claim 1: ZUR-2021-003411 — Workers Compensation
- **Date of loss:** 2021-04-12
- **Location:** Oakland HQ — Freezer aisle transition area
- **Cause:** Employee slipped on ice accumulation at the transition between freezer aisle B (-10F) and the ambient dock staging area. The floor drain at the transition point was partially blocked, allowing meltwater to refreeze.
- **Nature of injury:** Fractured left wrist (distal radius)
- **Indemnity:** 6 weeks lost time
- **Incurred:** $45,200 ($22,700 medical + $22,500 indemnity)
- **Paid:** $45,200
- **Status:** Closed (2021-08-14)
- **Subrogation:** None
- **Notes:** Post-incident investigation found inadequate floor drainage and lack of anti-slip matting at the thermal transition zone. Corrective actions implemented: floor drain cleared, anti-slip matting installed, and temperature transition signage posted. This loss was NOT disclosed on the current broker application.

- **Incurred:** $12,800
- **Paid:** $12,800

### Claim 3: ZUR-2022-001599 — General Liability (Property Damage)
- **Date of loss:** 2022-02-18
- **Location:** Third-party warehouse, Hayward, CA (leased overflow storage)

### Claim 4: ZUR-2022-004876 — Property / Inland Marine
- **Date of loss:** 2022-06-30
- **Location:** Oakland HQ — Freezer aisle A
- **Cause:** Forklift operator (Unit V013) impacted a ceiling-mounted evaporator unit while stacking pallets to maximum height. The impact fractured the refrigerant coil, releasing an estimated 120 pounds of R-404A refrigerant. Product integrity was maintained by backup refrigeration.

- **Paid:** $94,300
- **Status:** Closed (2022-09-20)

### Claim 7: ZUR-2023-005211 — Property / Spoilage
- **Date of loss:** 2023-10-12
- **Location:** Oakland HQ — Engine room / Freezer aisle B
- **Cause:** A failed gasket on the 150 HP low-stage ammonia compressor released an estimated 80 pounds of anhydrous ammonia (R-717) into the engine room. Gas detection activated at 25 ppm; emergency ventilation engaged. Facility was partially evacuated for approximately 4 hours. Freezer aisle B lost temperature control for approximately 6 hours, rising from -8F to 22F before backup systems restored cooling.
- **Product spoilage:** Approximately $180,000 in frozen food inventory was condemned due to temperature excursion. Equipment cleanup and re-commissioning costs: $432,500. Total $612,500.
- **Incurred:** $612,500
- **Paid:** $612,500
- **Status:** Closed (2024-05-10)
- **OSHA reporting:** Recordable — ammonia release above RQ (reportable quantity under EPA CERCLA). No injuries.
- **Corrective actions:** Gasket replacement program accelerated for all compressors. Redundant pressure monitoring installed. Weekly leak inspections implemented. This loss was NOT disclosed on the current broker application.
