    ========================================================================
                              ORDER
    ========================================================================

    IT IS HEREBY ORDERED as follows:

    1. CHILD SUPPORT. Petitioner, MICHAEL J. PATEL, M.D., shall pay to
       Respondent, LISA M. SULLIVAN (f/k/a Lisa M. Patel), child support
       in the amount of ONE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED DOLLARS ($1,500.00) per
       month, payable as follows:

       a. $750.00 per month per child (2 children)
       b. Payments due on the 1st day of each month
       c. First payment due: January 1, 2026
       d. Payments to continue until further order of court

    2. METHOD OF PAYMENT. Payments shall be made via direct deposit from
       Petitioner's account at Springfield Community Bank (Account ending
       in 7821) to Respondent's account at Chase Bank (Account ending in
       4521). Alternatively, payments may be made through the Illinois
       State Disbursement Unit (ISDU) if either party requests.

    3. INCOME WITHHOLDING. An immediate income withholding order shall
       issue to Petitioner's employer, Springfield Anesthesiology Group,
       P.C., 800 N. 1st Street, Springfield, IL 62702.

    4. TERMINATION. Child support shall terminate as to each child upon
       that child's emancipation, defined as: (a) reaching age 18 (or 19
       if still attending high school), (b) marriage, (c) death, (d) entry
       into active military service, or (e) otherwise emancipated by
       operation of law. Support shall be modifiable upon a showing of
       substantial change in circumstances.

    5. REIMBURSEMENT. Petitioner shall reimburse Respondent for child-related
       healthcare expenses not covered by insurance within 30 days of
       receipt of documentation.

    6. NO ALIMONY. The parties have waived any claim for spousal maintenance
       (alimony) as set forth in the Marital Settlement Agreement. No
       maintenance is payable by either party to the other.
