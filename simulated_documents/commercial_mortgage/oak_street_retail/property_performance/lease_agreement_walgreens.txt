THIS NET LEASE AGREEMENT (the "Lease") is made and entered into as of the
1st day of July, 2016, by and between OAK STREET RETAIL PROPERTIES LLC, an
Illinois limited liability company ("Landlord"), and WALGREEN CO., a
corporation organized under the laws of the State of Illinois ("Tenant").

   [Assignment noted: Original Landlord assigned all rights to

    of Lease attached as Exhibit J.]

================================================================================
ARTICLE 1 - BASIC LEASE PROVISIONS
================================================================================

1.1 Premises. Approximately 14,200 square feet of ground floor retail space,
together with the land underlying and adjacent thereto as more particularly
described on Exhibit A attached hereto (the "Premises").

1.2 Term. Twenty (20) years, commencing on the Commencement Date.

1.3 Commencement Date. July 1, 2016.

1.4 Expiration Date. June 30, 2036.

1.5 Renewal Options. Two (2) consecutive options to renew for five (5) years
each, exercisable by Tenant by written notice to Landlord not less than six
(6) months prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

1.6 Base Rent. [See Article 3 below]

1.7 Security Deposit. None.

1.8 Permitted Use. Operation of a retail pharmacy and related retail sales.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 2 - TRIPLE NET PROVISION
================================================================================

2.1 Net Lease. This is a triple net ("NNN") lease. Tenant shall pay all
costs, expenses, and charges of every kind and nature relating to the
Premises, including without limitation:

  (a) All real property taxes and assessments;
  (b) All insurance premiums;
  (c) All utilities, including electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash, and
      telecommunications;
  (d) All maintenance, repair, and replacement costs, including structural
      and roof; and
  (e) All janitorial, landscaping, snow removal, and parking lot maintenance.

2.2 Landlord Obligations. Landlord shall have no obligation to maintain,
repair, or replace any portion of the Premises. Landlord's sole obligation
is to convey the Premises to Tenant at lease commencement and to not
interfere with Tenant's quiet enjoyment.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 3 - RENT
================================================================================

3.1 Base Rent. Tenant shall pay to Landlord annual base rent as follows:

  Lease Years 1-5  (07/01/2016 - 06/30/2021):  $259,090.00 per year
  Lease Years 6-10 (07/01/2021 - 06/30/2026):  $285,000.00 per year
  Lease Years 11-15(07/01/2026 - 06/30/2031):  $313,500.00 per year
  Lease Years 16-20(07/01/2031 - 06/30/2036):  $344,850.00 per year

   [Margin note with arrow pointing to 2026-2031 row:]
   "Rent bump coming 07/01/2026! $313,500/yr = $26,125/mo.
    Current rent $23,750/mo. Underwrite at new rent? - JM"

3.2 Payment. Base Rent shall be paid in equal monthly installments in advance
on the first day of each month.

3.3 Late Fee. Any rent payment not received within five (5) days of the due
date shall be subject to a late fee of five percent (5%) of the delinquent
amount.

3.4 Rent Abatement. None.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 4 - TAXES AND INSURANCE
================================================================================

4.1 Real Property Taxes. Tenant shall pay all real property taxes and
assessments levied against the Premises. Tenant may contest any tax assessment
at Tenant's sole cost and expense.

4.2 Insurance. Tenant shall maintain, at Tenant's sole cost and expense:
  (a) Commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than
      $5,000,000 per occurrence;
  (b) Property insurance covering the full replacement cost of the Premises;
  (c) Such other insurance as Landlord may reasonably require.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 5 - CORPORATE GUARANTY
================================================================================

5.1 Guaranty. The obligations of Tenant under this Lease are guaranteed by
Walgreens Boots Alliance Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Guarantor"), pursuant
to a separate Corporate Guaranty executed contemporaneously herewith.

   [See Corporate Guaranty - Walgreens Boots Alliance Inc., dated 07/01/2016.
    Guaranty is unconditional and continuing.]

================================================================================
ARTICLE 6 - ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING
================================================================================

6.1 Tenant may assign this Lease or sublet all or any portion of the Premises
without Landlord's consent to any affiliate of Tenant, and to any successor
entity resulting from merger, acquisition, or corporate reorganization.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 7 - DEFAULT
================================================================================

7.1 Event of Default. The following shall constitute an event of default:
  (a) Failure to pay rent within ten (10) days after written notice;
  (b) Failure to perform any other obligation under this Lease for thirty (30)
      days after written notice;
  (c) Bankruptcy or insolvency of Tenant.

7.2 Remedies. Landlord may, upon default: terminate this Lease, re-enter the
Premises, accelerate rent, and pursue all legal remedies.
