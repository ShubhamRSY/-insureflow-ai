1.0 SITE DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

The subject property consists of a 5.8-acre vacant parcel located at the
northwest corner of River Road and County Highway 14 in Riverbend, IL.
The site is currently undeveloped agricultural land. The proposed use is
construction of a self-storage facility with ancillary retail space.

Site topography: Flat to gently sloping (2% grade east to west).
Surface cover: Currently fallow field - previously row crops.
Drainage: Site drains to county ditch along south boundary.

1.1 SURROUNDING LAND USE

  North: Single-family residential (subdivision - built 2018-2022)
  South: County Highway 14, beyond which is more agricultural land
  East: River Road, beyond which is Riverbend Commerce Park (light
        industrial/warehouse)
  West: Agricultural (farmland) - row crops

7.0 OPINION
================================================================================

It is the opinion of Prairie State Environmental Consultants that the subject
property is suitable for the proposed self-storage development. No further
environmental investigation is warranted at this time.

Note: The construction contractor will be required to obtain an IL EPA NPDES
General Permit for Stormwater Discharges from Construction Activities (ILR10)
and prepare a SWPPP prior to site disturbance.
