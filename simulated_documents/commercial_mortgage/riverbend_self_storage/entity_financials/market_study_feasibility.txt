1.0 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This study analyzes the feasibility of a proposed 65,000 SF self-storage
facility (approximately 500 units) with 5,000 SF of ancillary retail space
(moving truck rental, packing supplies) to be located at 1800 River Road,
Riverbend, IL 62788.

CONCLUSION: THE PROPOSED PROJECT IS FEASIBLE AND SUPPORTED BY MARKET DEMAND.

6.0 RETAIL COMPONENT ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

The proposed 5,000 SF retail component (moving truck rental, packing supplies)
is expected to generate approximately $85,000-$95,000/yr in incremental
revenue with minimal incremental operating cost. Tenant improvement costs
are estimated at $40/SF.

================================================================================
7.0 CONCLUSION
================================================================================

The Riverbend trade area demonstrates strong demographic trends, rapid
population growth, and a complete absence of self-storage supply. The
proposed 65,000 SF facility would serve a clearly unmet need. Absorption
projections are achievable, and the project economics are sound.

SMR recommends proceeding with development.
